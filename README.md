📊 E-Commerce Revenue & Customer Analytics Case Study

🚀 Executive Summary

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to identify revenue leakage, customer value segments, and growth opportunities.

The analysis reveals that:

A small segment of customers drives a disproportionate share of revenue
There are significant drop-offs in the purchase funnel
Customer retention declines rapidly after the first purchase

👉 These insights translate directly into targeted retention, funnel optimization, and revenue growth strategies

🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses often struggle with:

Low customer retention
Poor visibility into high-value customers
Inefficient conversion funnels

This project addresses:

“Where are we losing revenue, and how can we increase customer lifetime value?”

🧠 Analytical Approach
1. Customer Segmentation (RFM)
Identified high-value, loyal, and at-risk customers
Quantified customer contribution to revenue
2. Funnel Analysis
Tracked customer journey from order to completion
Identified key drop-off stages
3. Cohort Analysis
Measured retention trends over time
Evaluated repeat purchase behavior
4. Sales & Revenue Analysis
Monthly revenue trends
Category-level performance
Order behavior patterns

📊 Key Insights

💰 Revenue Concentration
Top customer segments generate the majority of revenue
Long tail of low-value customers contributes minimally

⚠️ Funnel Leakage
Significant drop-offs occur in early purchase stages
Indicates friction in decision-making or checkout process

📉 Weak Retention
Sharp decline in repeat purchases after first transaction
Suggests lack of post-purchase engagement strategy

🛍️ Product-Level Patterns
Certain categories consistently drive repeat purchases
Others contribute only to one-time sales


💡 Business Recommendations

1. Target High-Value Customers
Loyalty programs
Personalized offers
Priority engagement strategies
2. Fix Funnel Drop-Offs
Simplify checkout experience
Improve pricing transparency
Reduce decision friction
3. Improve Retention
Post-purchase engagement (email, offers)
Re-targeting campaigns
Subscription or repeat incentives
4. Focus on High-Performing Categories
Prioritize marketing spend
Optimize inventory strategy
